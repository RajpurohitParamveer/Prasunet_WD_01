Porsche 911 GT3 RS

Description

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a high-performance sports car renowned for its track-focused design and engineering excellence. It features a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine producing 518 horsepower, enabling it to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.0 seconds and reach a top speed of 193 mph. The car's advanced aerodynamics, lightweight construction, and precise handling make it a top choice for driving enthusiasts. With its bold design and racing-inspired features, the GT3 RS offers a thrilling driving experience both on and off the track.

Specifications

  • Engine: 4.0L naturally aspirated flat-six
  • Horsepower: 518 HP
  • 0-60 mph: 3.0 seconds
  • Top Speed: 193 mph
  • Transmission: 7-speed PDK
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive
  • Weight: 1,450 kg (3,196 lbs)
  • Price: Starting at $187,500
